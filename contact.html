We currently provide door-to-door delivery across Sibu, Miri, and Bintulu.

Yes! You can contact your nearest branch via WhatsApp to check stock and arrange a quick pickup at any of our physical store.

For local deliveries within our branch cities, we typically fulfill orders within 24-48 hours depending on stock availability.
